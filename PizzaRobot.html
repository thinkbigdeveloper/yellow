Steps for a Robot who's job is to make pizzas:

  1. Ask customer (determine) style of pizza
  2. Ask customer (determine) size of pizza
  3. Ask customer (determine) toppings of pizza
  4. Confirm order
  5. Request payment
  6. Confirm payment or give change (as required)
  7. Assemble pizza
  8. Cook pizza
  9. Package pizza
  10. Deliver pizza to customer
